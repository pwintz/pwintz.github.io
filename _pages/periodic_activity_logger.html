--- layout: single permalink: /tools/activity_log/ title: Periodic Activity Logger --- This page is designed to help you keep track of your activities and remind you of what you should be doing. It allows you to log your current activity, set a delay for the next activity, and provide suggestions for what to do next. To get started, please select a Markdown file where you want to record your activities. Content will be appended to the end of the file.
