<div class="lvl2"><h2>C. GRADUATE STUDENT RESEARCH TRAINEES AND GRADUATE STUDENT RESEARCH FELLOWS</h2>

<div class="lvl3"><h3 class="inline-header">1.</h3> The wages provided to Graduate Student Research Trainees and Graduate Student Research Fellows are determined by their training grants or external fellowships. Trainees and Fellows shall continue to receive wages from their training grants or external fellowships through the local systems currently in place.
</div><!-- End of level 3: 1.-->
<div class="lvl3"><h3 class="inline-header">2.</h3> The University shall transition Trainees and Fellows to the <span class="tooltip">GSR<span class="tooltip-text"><b>Graduate student researcher</b>: {% assign tooltip_term = site.data.union_glossary | where: "Term", "graduate student researcher" %}{{ tooltip_term[0]["Meaning"] }}</span></span> Scale effective October 1, 2023.
<div class="lvl4"><h4 class="inline-header">a.</h4> When extramural agencies establish stipends at a rate less than the University established wage minimum, and the campus elects to proceed with the appointment of a <span class="tooltip">GSR<span class="tooltip-text"><b>Graduate student researcher</b>: {% assign tooltip_term = site.data.union_glossary | where: "Term", "graduate student researcher" %}{{ tooltip_term[0]["Meaning"] }}</span></span>, the campus shall provide additional funding to increase the wage rate to the next highest salary point on the GSR scale, if applicable.
</div><!-- End of level 4: a.-->
<div class="lvl4"><h4 class="inline-header">b.</h4> The University shall create a job code to provide a supplemental <span class="tooltip">GSR<span class="tooltip-text"><b>Graduate student researcher</b>: {% assign tooltip_term = site.data.union_glossary | where: "Term", "graduate student researcher" %}{{ tooltip_term[0]["Meaning"] }}</span></span> appointment to provide for the additional funding.
</div><!-- End of level 4: b.-->
<div class="lvl4"><h4 class="inline-header">c.</h4> Trainees and Fellows shall also be eligible for experience-based progression pursuant to subsection B.3. above.

</div><!-- End of level 2: C. GRADUATE STUDENT RESEARCH TRAINEES AND GRADUATE STUDENT RESEARCH FELLOWS-->
